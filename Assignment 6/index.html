Meet our Chef

As long as I can remember, I have had a passion for excellent food. I began cooking at the age of four in my grandmother’s kitchen. She taught me that the simple food can be spectacular if done with talent and precision. My style reflects that time-honored approach. I call it simply great cuisine..

He has been a talented, trendsetting chef for more than 35 years. He has formal training from the Culinary Institute of America and Ecole Hoteliere in Switzerland and holds a bachelor’s degree in Hotel Restaurant Management from the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Chef has worked in numerous upscale establishments, including The Mirage in Las Vegas, The Marriott Marquis in New York City and Radisson Hotels & Resorts. He has opened several successful restaurants and was the Executive Chef at the four-star Omni Hotel in Richmond.
